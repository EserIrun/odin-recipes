Ceaser Potato Casserole Recipe

Description

This Caesar potato casserole is scalloped potatoes with a lively twist. Potato slices are baked in a creamy, lemony sauce reminiscent of Caesar salad, with plenty of Parmesan cheese in between.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Milk, Parmesan Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Onion
  • Mastard
  • Olive Oil
  • Potato

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Melt butter and mix it with Garlic, flour, cook
  3. Line bottom of prepared dish & sprinkle parmesans
  4. Cook the Casserole
  5. Add the seasonings
