Lahmacun Recipe

Description

This Turkish-style pizza is a Turkish flat bread layered with a fresh sauce composed of tomatoes, sweet bell peppers, and minced lamb cooked with a flavorful selection of Mediterranean spices.

Ingredients

  • Ground lamb
  • Chopped Basil & Parsley
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Flour&Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Make a dough
  2. Mix the ingredients
  3. Heat the oven
  4. Stir the mixing with dough
  5. Cook the Lahmacuns
